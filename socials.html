Community Expectations

By attending our events you agree to:

  • Make room for newcomers and different perspectives
  • Explain jargon and acronyms
  • Don't interrupt or dominate discussions
  • Stay open to disagreement
  • Unwanted sexual attention, aggression, or demeaning behaviour will lead to removal

If you'd like to reach out about a community health concern please email community (at) caish (dot) org or you can use our anonymous form.

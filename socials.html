Community Expectations

Be considerate

  • Be mindful of how your words and actions affect others.
  • Donâ€™t interrupt, dominate discussions, or shame others.
  • Make room for newcomers and different perspectives to yours.

Keep the space accessible

  • Avoid unnecessary jargon; explain acronyms and references.
  • Value epistemic humility and openness to disagreement.
  • Don't make assumptions about people's gender, background, or expertise.

Harm isn't tolerated

  • Unwanted sexual attention, aggression, or belittling behaviour is not acceptable.
  • Don't make others feel small, excluded, or unsafe.
  • Patterns of harmful behavior will lead to removal from the space.
